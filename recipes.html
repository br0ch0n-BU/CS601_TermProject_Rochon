Kale Mango Smoothie

I usually drink this smoothie every morning. It's very tasty and refreshing! If you don't like eating your greens, this is a good way to get your daily dose, as the fruit dominates the flavor of this smoothie. Adding some flavorless protein powder further fortifies the natural nutrition provided by the fruits and vegetables.

I get all ingredients from my local Costco, including the blender itself, the super-powerful Vitamix 5200.

Ingredient Approximate amount
Power greens 2 handfuls
Baby carrots 1 half handful
Banana 1
Greek yogurt 2 cups
Collagen powder 1 scoop
Frozen mango chunks 1.5 cups
Water 16 fl oz

Put all ingredients into the blender and blend on high until smooth and creamy:

Smoked Brisket

Nothing's yummier than a slow smoked beef brisket with a tasty crispy bark! It's very easy to do provided you own a smoker. The only challenges are the waiting time and bringing enough friends over to eat the massive pile of meat.

You can eat it sliced with BBQ sauce or make little sliders using bread rolls.

This recipe is taken mostly from Traeger, though I only sometimes use the foil.

Ingredient Approximate amount
Packer brisket 1 whole. 10-20 lbs.
Tony Cachere's Creole Seasoning a lot

Trim the most absurd fat from the brisket, but leave plenty for flavor! Slather both sides of the brisket with olive oil and seasoning. Wrap the brisket in plastic wrap and let chill in the fridge overnight. The next day, heat smoker to 225° and throw on grill, fat side up for approximately 10 hours or until probe reads 204°. Slice and enjoy!
